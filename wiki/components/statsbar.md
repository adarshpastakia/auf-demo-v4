#### `ui-statsbar`
Statsbar

* Attributes
  * `small`
  * `vertical`
  * `icon-only`: Apply text theme to icon only
  * `{theme}`: Statsbar theme
  * `{background theme}`: Statsbar background theme

* Theme colors
  * `default`
  * `dark`
  * `muted`
  * `primary`
  * `secondary`
  * `info`
  * `danger`
  * `success`
  * `warning`

* Background Theme colors
  * `default-bg`
  * `dark-bg`
  * `muted-bg`
  * `primary-bg`
  * `secondary-bg`
  * `info-bg`
  * `danger-bg`
  * `success-bg`
  * `warning-bg`
